Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup whole masoor daal
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp oil for cooking
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Some coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • 3-4 curry leaves
Preparation:
  • Wash masoor daal and boil in a pressure cooker for 3 whistles.
  • Cut and blend the onion to make paste.
  • Prepare ginger-garlic paste.
  • Puree the tomatoes.
  • Slit green chillies into long thin pieces
  • Wash and chop cilantro
Steps:
  1. In a wok, heat oil and add onion paste. Fry till it is light brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chiliies.
  3. Once the raw aroma goes away, add red chilli powder, salt, turmeric powder, coriander powder, pepper and 1 tbsp water. Cook on a high flame for 30 seconds.
  4. Add tomato puree. Cook for 4-5 minutes on medium flame. Now add 1 cup water and cook for 10 minutes on high flame.
  5. Add the boiled masoor lentils to the mixture and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Now add 1.5 cups water and chopped cillantro. Cook for another 10 minutes on medium flame.
  7. In a pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds and curry leaves.
  8. Add this ghee to the prepared daal.
  9. Ready to serve.
